Beaker unlocks the potential of the Peer-to-Peer Web

User control

Frontend applications are decoupled from backend services, so users maintain control of their data, and can't be locked in by service providers.

Collaboration and experimentation built-in

Any site can be forked. With one click, you can duplicate an application and tailor it to be your own.

Publishing without relying on hosting providers

Hosting is instant and free. Upload content from within Beaker and peers on the network will rehost it for you.
