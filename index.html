Our mission is to advance the Web Platform by working outside the standards process. Here are our current experiments:

1. The Dat Protocol

Dat is a peer-to-peer hypermedia protocol that can host sites directly from the browser. Its Web APIs enable users to publish content independently of a host, and the security features enable secret sharing.

Learn more

2. Builtin Ad-blocker

Modern ad-networks slow down sites, track users, and introduce malware. Beaker will block ads automatically for speed and security.
